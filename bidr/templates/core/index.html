What is Bidr?

A cloud-based platform for silent auctions which includes a mobile client and administrative backend. Bidr aims to bring silent auctions into the age of technology.

Bid on Items

Auction attendees are easily able to bid on items without ever having to get out of their chairs.

Mobile Friendly

Attendees may have any manner of mobile device, thus we provide Mobile Web support. For those with an Apple product, we also have an iOS App.

Web Security

Bidr is fully protected with 256-bit SSL encryption. Rest assured that your data is secure.
